User Persona

  • Name: Paul Pierce
  • Age: 34
  • Job: Help Desk
  • Location: Rio Rancho, NM

User Story

  • Paul Pierce is a resident of Rio Rancho, NM who is very concerned about this pandemic for the fact that his relatives are older and more at risk to this disease, thus wanting to be knowledgeable so he can inform others around him, potentially preventing the loss of a loved one for him or someone he knows.

Used Devices

  • iPhone 11
  • Windows 10 Intel Based Desktop
  • OSX Based Laptop

Scenarios Users Will Use This Site/App

  • First and foremost to be more informed about the COVID-19 situation pertaining to specific area in general.
  • To find testing areas closet to their location
  • To interact and share information with other people in your state/county. Peer2Peer connection.
  • To be able to gain information about which local business' are exercising practices that allow customers to conduct business in the safest fashion possible. I.E.: If certain business' are offering curb side pick ups on items.
Frustrations That May Be Exhibited Using This Site/App
  • Slight possibility there might not be exact information for their exact area.
User Interaction Flow
  • User will navigate to the site and be greeted by the home page which will have information displayed as well as a navigation menu (all subject to wireframe design).
  • User will decide from one of the multiple features the site offers and navigate to the menu pertaining to that topic.
  • User has the option of creating an account that way if user finds helpful resources they can bookmark them all in one convenient spot that saves through their profile.
Site/App Goals
  • The goal of this Site/App is to provide accurate relevant information pertaining this pandemic while also potentially providing new innovative features that other similar Sites/Apps haven't implemented.
